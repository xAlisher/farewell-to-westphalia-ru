# Style guide

## Goal

Clear, readable Russian that is conceptually accurate.

## Avoid filler

Avoid bureaucratic or academic filler such as: "в целях", "в рамках", "осуществление", "производить".
Prefer direct verbs and concrete phrasing.

## Sentence length

Prefer short sentences and natural cadence. Split long or tangled sentences.

## Glossary

The glossary is authoritative. Use terms from docs/glossary.yml consistently.

## New terms

When a new term appears, add it to docs/glossary.yml with:
- the preferred Russian term
- forbidden variants
- optional notes

## Punctuation

Use "-" instead of "—".
